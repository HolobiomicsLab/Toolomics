# Contributing to toolomics

Thank you for your interest in contributing.

For architecture and development workflow details, see `docs/CONTRIBUTING.md`.

## Licensing and Contributor Agreement

This repository is publicly distributed under the GNU Affero General Public License v3.0 (AGPLv3).

All non-trivial external contributions require a signed Contributor License Agreement (CLA) before they can be merged. Depending on your situation, maintainers may require:

- an Individual CLA; or
- a Corporate CLA / employer authorization.

By submitting a contribution, you confirm that you have the legal right to submit it.

Accepted contributions may be distributed in the public AGPLv3 repository and may also be used in a separate commercial licensing track by the sole rights-holder, Centre national de la recherche scientifique (CNRS), as described in the applicable CLA.

If you are contributing in the course of employment or on behalf of an institution/company, contact dr20.spv@listes.cnrs.fr before (or when) opening a pull request.

## CLA Workflow in Pull Requests

- Non-trivial external pull requests require a signed CLA before merge.
- You may be asked to complete either an Individual CLA or a Corporate CLA / employer authorization.
- The CLA Assistant Lite workflow will prompt first-time contributors directly in the PR thread/checks.
- The `CLA Check` status check must pass before merge.
- Contact: dr20.spv@listes.cnrs.fr
